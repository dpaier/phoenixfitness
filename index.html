It is our passion to not only assist, but also educate and direct you with fueling your unique body metabolism based on the prevailing science on the subject matter. Phoenix Fitness manifested upon the principles of Epigenetics as they pertain to the fitness industry. Simply put - both exercise and nutrition effectively change how your body expresses its DNA. The systemic chemical reactions from that breakdown and absorption of food varies marginally from person to person and ultimately, the outcome varies drastically as an end product. Weight gain and preventable disease are just two examples of how passive abuse can lead to something detrimental if gone unnoticed. To address these concerns, our services provide a comprehensive, science based progression system. This ensures an efficient, safe and calculated approach to achieving your fitness goals!
